What this calculator is, and isn't This is a diagnostic, not a quote. The math is deliberately transparent so any CFO can verify it in five minutes. Spread defaults are conservative midpoints of the published bank ranges; your actual bank rates may differ. The "what if you'd held" panel uses historical USD/INR averages and is illustrative of operational hedging value; it is not a forecast and does not constitute investment advice.
